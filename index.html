Jordan Lewis

Front-End Developer

Thanks for visiting my page! I'm Jordan, a Front-End Web Developer based in Columbus, Ohio. That's me pictured with my wife on top of Clingman's Dome. Unfortunately, the mist that day made visibility low.


As a Front-End Developer, I specialize in HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and Wordpress. I pride myself on building user-friendly applications with beautiful user interfaces. I love building websites because of the thrill one gets when you slowly see your vision come together in a place accessible to everyone in the world. I like the logic of React and JavaScript in piecing together different functions and methods to get the site to work how you want it to work. I aspire to build my skillset everyday to obtain my ultimate goal of becoming a Full-Stack Developer. Feel free to check out some of my projects below!

Professionally, I spent two and a half years as a Human Resources Coordinator at a local grocery chain, Giant Eagle. It is there where I learned soft skills such as communication, time management, professionalism in the workplace, and cooperation amongst co-workers in different sectors. But that's not what intrigued you about this section.


In between my time as a Human Resources Coordinator and a Front-End Developer, I made my living building stacks of chips like the one depicted by playing poker. Playing poker professionally taught me quite a few lessons that are critical to web development. These include:


  • There are many different ways to get the same result.
  • How to piece together an accurate picture based on incomplete information.
  • What looks like a "minor" improvement adds up over time.
  • You almost never make the big mistake. Instead, it's the small one you should look for.
